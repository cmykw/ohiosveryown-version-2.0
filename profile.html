Profile

Ohio's Very Own is my personal creative and design initiative where I make things with pixels and paths; specifically things involving visual design, UI, brand identity, illustration, print, and other cool things.

I currently reside in the greater Atlanta working as a visual designer at Bluetube LLC, a digital agency. I also moonlight at Eleven Warriors which gives me an oppurtunity to combine two things I love: sports and design. I also mess around with some front-end stuff over at Codepen. I have a strong passion for good design, but I do more than just work.

In my free time I enjoy playing basketball, distance running, and being outdoors. I'm a huge supporter of the U.S. Men's National Soccer Team and The Ohio State University - Go Buckeyes.
